ABOUT Interstellar

Interstellar is a movie directed by Christopher Nolan where Cooper has to go into space to save humanity. Due to a global food crisis, astronauts are sent to different planets to explore for different habital planets. Cooper faces several challenges as he travels against space and time away from his family, trying to save humanity.
